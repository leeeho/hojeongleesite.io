Hojeong Lee graduated from Seoul National University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Industrial Design, and currently is a researcher at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology.
She is interested in artificially animated machines, environments, and computers as continuum of lives. She seeks emotional bonds between them and humans.
Her research interest lies in HCI and HRI. She is currently conducting research in the fields of wearable haptic device, soft actuators, and ergonomics for XR.
